System reads as high-contrast monochrome (pure black on white) with a single warm neutral (`#D7CECC` taupe footer) and earthy photographic backdrops (rust/red-rock desert tones) providing the only color. [unverified] Light-grey/teal section tints seen in `full.png` are not in the sampled palette — only the literal `section` background `#000000` is captured; the visible teal band may be a nested element.

**Type:**
- Single family throughout: `"ES Allianz", Arial, sans-serif` (body, H1, p all share it).
- Scale (sampled): H1 **152px**, body **16px**, paragraph **12px** — an extreme display-to-body ratio (~12.7x) driving the cinematic hero. Headings are weighty and tightly set.

**Density / grid:** Generous whitespace; alternating full-bleed cinematic image sections and white/light content sections. Content sections use bento/multi-tile grids (5-tile capability grid, 2-up persona cards, 3-up metric cards). [unverified] exact column counts/gutters not in artifacts — inferred from `full.png`.

**Depth / border treatment:** Cards sit on light backgrounds with soft rounded corners and subtle shadows (see persona and metric cards in `full.png`). Buttons are solid black pills with white text. [unverified] corner-radius/shadow values not sampled.

**Imagery style:** Photographic and heavy — 101 `<img>`, 67 SVG, 7 video, 0 canvas. Signature aesthetic is cinematic sci-fi/space photography (astronauts on Mars-like desert and red-rock canyon) for hero and "Listen to Julian"; realistic human portraits for the Alice/Julian persona cards; small product-UI screenshots inside capability tiles. No hero video or canvas (`heroHasVideo: false`, `heroHasCanvas: false`).

## 4. Motion + implementation

**Animation stack (from network + globals evidence in JSON):**
- Globals present: `gsap`, `Webflow`, `SplitType`.
- Network scripts loaded:
  - `https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/gsap/3.12.5/gsap.min.js`
  - `https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/gsap/3.12.5/ScrollTrigger.min.js`
  - `https://unpkg.com/split-type@0.3.4/umd/index.min.js`
- Verdict: **GSAP 3.12.5 + ScrollTrigger + SplitType on a Webflow-hosted site.** Animation approach is confirmed by both globals and network artifacts.

**Inferred effects (technique mapped to the loaded libraries):**
- **Split-text reveals** — SplitType breaks the large headlines (notably the 152px hero H1) into chars/words/lines so GSAP can stagger their entrance. Restraint/trigger timing [unverified] (the libraries' presence is verified; the specific tween parameters are not in the artifacts).
- **Scroll-triggered section reveals / pinning** — ScrollTrigger drives on-scroll fades, parallax over the full-bleed photographic backdrops, and likely pinned/scrubbed transitions between the cinematic image sections and content sections. [unverified] exact scrub/pin config not captured.
- **Rotating top-bar messages** — the announcement ribbon cycles ("Slide 2 of 2" in gist) implying an auto-advancing slider; [unverified] whether GSAP or Webflow's native slider drives it.
- **Webflow interactions (IX2)** — the `Webflow` global indicates baseline Webflow-authored interactions may also be present alongside GSAP.

This is a motion-forward site (dedicated split-text + scroll engine), not a static page.
